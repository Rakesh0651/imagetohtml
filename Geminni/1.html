Comparing AI Frameworks

Framework Strengths Weaknesses
TensorFlow Highly scalable, open-source Steep learning curve for beginners
PyTorch Flexible, easy to debug Slower compared to TensorFlow in production
Keras Simple to use, high-level API Less flexibility in complex models

AI in Creative Industries

AI is also pushing the boundaries in the creative sector. Artists are using AI tools to generate artwork, music, and even novels. For example, the DeepArt algorithm transforms photos into art by learning the styles of famous painters.
